== Description ==

= Safeguard Your WooCommerce Store. =

SaferCheckout (Lite) is a powerful security plugin to protect your WooCommerce store from fraudulent activities. It offers several unique and advanced features as well as customizable rules to filter every order on your online store: email address, IP address, geolocation, order, customer, payment method and many more.

As usual with all our WordPress plugins, you can be sure of its robustness, reliability, secure code and its compliance with privacy laws such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

Protecting your WooCommerce store has never been so easy!

= Features =

* High-Performance Order Storage (HPOS) compatibility.
* Block-based checkout compatibility.
* Lightweight plugin: No additional database tables created.
* Customizable fraud risk levels.
* Multiple whitelists: IP address (IPv4, IPv6, AS number), email address, payment method, repeat or recurring customers.
* Multiple blacklists: IP address (IPv4, IPv6, AS number), Reverse DNS (Pro version), email address, shipping & billing address, user & company name.
* Advanced detection (Pro version):  Email MX/A/AAAA records check, email domain registration check, IP address rDNS check, IP address DNSBL check.
* Geolocation.
* Suspicious bot detection (Pro version).
* Configuration import & export (Pro version).
* Simulation mode.
* Caching (Pro version).
* GDPR compliant.


Need more security? Take the time to explore the Pro version: [SaferCheckout Pro](https://nintechnet.com/safercheckout/)

= Requirements for running SaferCheckout (Lite) =

* WordPress 6.0.3 or above.
* PHP 8.1 or above.
* WooCommerce 7.0.0 or above

= Privacy Policy =

SaferCheckout (Lite) doesn't collect any private data from you or your visitors, and doesn't use cookies either. You can install and use it on your website in compliance with privacy laws such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).


== Installation ==

1. Upload the ZIP file from the WordPress "Plugins > Add New" menu.
2. Activate the plugin.
3. To access the plugin, click on the "WooCommerce > Settings > SaferCheckout Lite".
